How to play

1

Aim your shooter by dragging it left or right to align with the target bubbles

2

Shoot bubbles to match three or more of the same color to pop them and free the trapped birds

3

Strategically plan your shots to create combos and chain reactions for higher scores

4

Clear obstacles like blocks and stones by popping bubbles around them

5

Use special bubbles, like bombs or rainbow bubbles, to clear large sections of the board

6

Complete level objectives, such as freeing a certain number of birds or reaching a specific score

7

Collect stars by completing levels with high scores, unlocking new stages and challenges

8

Participate in daily events and challenges to earn additional rewards and power-ups
